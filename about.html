About Our Trust

The founder of Sasti Velan Universal Foundation is a socially committed leader with a
deep passion for uplifting underprivileged communities and promoting holistic well-being.
With a vision to create a more compassionate and inclusive society, the founder established the foundation to serve the public without distinction of race, caste, religion, or social status.
Their mission is to provide relief to the poor, offer career guidance and development for youth,
promote awareness of health and organic living, and support those affected by natural disasters.
The founder is also dedicated to preserving traditional knowledge, such as astrology and Ayurveda, while encouraging modern approaches to social welfare. With a focus on healthcare, education, and environmental awareness, the foundation aims to empower individuals and strengthen communities. bilitation homes for the elderly.

Cuddalore

Sasti Velan Universal Foundation

We are dedicated to helping poor families and students by providing support for their education, enabling them to pursue their dreams and build a brighter future. Our motivational speeches and awareness campaigns inspire individuals to overcome challenges and aim for success, while also offering assistance to families in need. In times of disaster, we stand by those affected, offering relief and hope. Additionally, we regularly donate food to poor families, ensuring they have the nourishment they need to survive and thrive.
Through these efforts, we strive to make a lasting impact on communities in need..

Madurai

Sasti Velan Universal Foundation

In Madurai, we focus on uplifting marginalized communities by providing essential resources for their well-being. Our initiatives aim to empower individuals through access to healthcare, education, and skill development programs.
We are committed to creating a strong sense of community, where people can come together to share knowledge, build self-reliance, and promote sustainable living. By supporting local projects and offering aid during tough times, we strive to improve the quality of life and create opportunities for everyone, ensuring no one is left behind.
